Spinach Pie Ingredients

All amounts can be adjusted to fit your preferences! You can also scale for different skillet sizes, or to achieve different ratios of crust to filling. I generally don't measure anything when I cook this and it always turns out well, if slightly different each time.

  • 1T minced garlic
  • 1 small or Â½ large onion, diced
  • 2T olive oil, plus more for brushing on top
  • 16oz frozen chopped spinach
  • 2t dried dill
  • black pepper to taste
  • 8oz crumbled feta
  • 5 eggs
  • 1 can of crescent rolls

Equipment

  • large cast iron skillet
    • I think mine is 11 inches
    • you could try this with another stove-to-oven safe skillet or dutch oven
  • wooden spoon
  • mixing bowl
  • fork for whisking eggs, the feta would get stuck in a wire whisk
  • pastry brush for the optional olive oil at the end
  • knife and cutting board
